📖 About the Odds column

The Odds column shows each team's estimated chance of winning the National Championship, displayed as 1 in X. These come from DraftKings Sportsbook as of Selection Sunday and reflect the betting market's collective wisdom — accounting for seed, strength of schedule, injuries, and historical trends. A team showing 1 in 4 is a heavy favorite; a team at 1 in 5,000 is a major long shot but not impossible — upsets are what March Madness is all about. The ⭐ badge marks potential Cinderella teams (seeds 12–16) worth watching for upsets.

📖 How to read these odds

Odds show each team's estimated chance of winning the National Championship, based on DraftKings Sportsbook as of Selection Sunday.

They are shown as 1 in X — meaning roughly 1 chance in X that this team wins it all. A lower number means a stronger favorite.

1 in 4 — Heavy favorite (e.g. Duke)
1 in 50 — Reasonable dark horse
1 in 1000+ — Major long shot
